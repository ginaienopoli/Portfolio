About

Inside Gina Ienopoli's story

GINA IENOPOLI

A solution solving individual who loves DESIGN and INTERACTIVE MEDIA. She sets up many goals for herself and hopes to be working in the industry as a diligent worker.

THE STORY of Gina was she grew up on Long Island in a surburban town for most of her life. Her family was very artistic which allowed her to learn and grow up to be an artist. Battling with a mental illness she would create art to relieve and express herself, which can be seen with her motifs of dark and surreal work. She would later go to the Fashion Institue of Technology for her Associate's in Illustration and her Bachelor's in Animation and Interactive Media.

THE SKILLS Gina posseses are traditional and digital. From a young she took traditional classes, yet growing up with technology has influenced her to go into digital art. At age 13 she picked her camera and used photoshop for editing. Later on she would advance her photoshop skills to digitally paint, and learn more software to advance her art.
