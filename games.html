DINBO

A 2D platformer game using unity! Use the left click on the mouse to shoot, arrows to move, and 'e' to pick up items!

Dinbo was a group project made in an intro to unity class. Very proud of my team, Ming Li and Karrlem Lewis, for pulling off such a beautiful game! I made the spikes, enemy animation, debugged code, and coded for the music and sound effects. Click the title to pop out a new window to play the game!
